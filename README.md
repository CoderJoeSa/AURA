# 🌟 Aurora – My Infinite Voice

### ✨ Personal Legacy Virtual Assistant

**Aurora** is a project that aims to transcend physical life by creating a realistic virtual assistant encapsulating my knowledge, love, companionship, teachings, and life vision. Its purpose is for my loved ones to converse with me and receive my support even when I am no longer physically present.

---

## 📖 **Description**

Aurora – My Infinite Voice is a virtual assistant trained on my personal texts, audio recordings, and reflections. It uses advanced natural language processing models and voice cloning technology to:

- Respond in my tone, style, and philosophy  
- Share advice, stories, and teachings  
- Provide companionship and motivation in meaningful moments

---

## 💻 **Planned Technologies**

- **NLP Model**: GPT-4, Mistral, or LLaMa 3 (fine-tuned with personal dataset)
- **Embeddings**: Pinecone or Weaviate for semantic search
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: ElevenLabs or Microsoft Custom Neural Voice
- **Backend**: Node.js + Express or Django
- **Frontend**: React Native (mobile app) or Next.js (web)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL or MongoDB
- **Infrastructure**: AWS or Azure
